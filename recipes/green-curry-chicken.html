Thai Green Curry Chicken

Description

Your kitchen will come alive with the fragrance and delicate balance of sweet and spicy flavors! The tender chicken bathed in a flavorful, creamy Green Curry sauce is best served over Jasmine rice. Best of all, this recipe, that the whole family will love, only takes 1 hour of your time!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 2 green onions chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  • 2 teaspoon fresh ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 cup cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Toss the chicken with 1 tablespoon soy sauce.
  2. Add the flour and coat the chicken pieces evenly.
  3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  4. Cook the chicken aobut 5 minutes or until brown.
  5. Remove the chicken from the skillet.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium and stir in the curry paste.
  7. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  8. Add the green onions, garlic, and ginger.
  9. Cook another 2 minutes.
  10. Return the chicken to the skillet.
  11. Stir and coat the chicken evenly with the curry mixture.
  12. Add the coconut milk, fish sauce, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, and sugar.
  13. Stir to combine.
  14. Simmer over medium heat for 20 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  15. Service over Jasmine rice and garnished with Cilantro.
